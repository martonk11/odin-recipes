Lasagna

This is a proper recipe for lasagna

Easz and delicious



Ingredients

  • 1 (3 ounce) package pancetta, minced
  • ½ cup diced carrot
  • ½ cup diced yellow onion
  • 6 ounces ground pork


Steps

  1. season pork
  2. fry pork
  3. put pancetta in cookware
  4. add pork
  5. cook for 20 minutes
